What we do

The Function Gym is not like your ordinary commercial gym it is so much more. In this gym you get a real sense of community and will build relationships and friendships from the get go. We have everything you need to succeed and reach your goals. We combine weight lifting, anaerobic and aerobic exercise to add a variety to your training. All classes are scaleable and adaptable to involve everyoen no matter your fitness or skill level.
